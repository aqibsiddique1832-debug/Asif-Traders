FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas?
We deliver across Navi Mumbai, Mumbai, Thane, and surrounding areas including Airoli, Rabale, Vashi, Kopar Khairane, and more. Contact us to confirm delivery to your location.
What is the minimum order quantity?
Minimum order quantities vary by product. For cement, minimum is 50 bags. For TMT bars, minimum is 100 kg. Contact us for specific product requirements.
Do you provide GST invoices?
Yes, we provide complete GST invoices for all purchases. We accept both cash and digital payments with proper billing.
What brands do you stock?
We stock genuine ISI-marked products from UltraTech, ACC, Ambuja (cement), TATA Steel, JSW Steel (TMT/bars), Kajaria, Somany (tiles), and other leading manufacturers.
Can I get credit facility?
We offer credit facilities for regular customers and established contractors. Terms are discussed on a case-by-case basis after initial orders.
Do you arrange transport for bulk orders?
Yes, we arrange door-to-door delivery across our service area. Delivery charges depend on location and order quantity. Same/next day delivery available for in-stock items.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Bank Transfer, and Credit/Debit Cards. For regular customers, we offer credit terms.
Are your products ISI certified?
All our cement, steel, and structural products are ISI-certified. We only deal in genuine, quality-verified building materials from authorized dealers.
